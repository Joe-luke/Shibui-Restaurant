Make a Reservation
Indulge in an authentic Japanese dining experience like no other, where every dish tells a story of craftsmanship and passion.
Our master chefs meticulously craft each plate, honoring the art of traditional Japanese cuisine while infusing innovative twists that awaken the palate.
At Shibui, we invite you to savor the delicate dance of flavors, from exquisitely fresh sashimi that melts in your mouth to the artistry of sushi rolls that are as delightful to behold as they are to taste.
Immerse yourself in the umami-rich depths of miso soups, the satisfying crunch of tempura, and the sizzle of hibachi-grilled delights.
Located in the heart of Nairobi's Kileleshwa, Shibui offers a tranquil escape from the bustling city life.
Our thoughtfully designed ambiance combines modern elegance with traditional Japanese elements, creating a space that invites you to unwind and relish every moment.
Embark on a culinary adventure that transcends borders and brings the spirit of Japan to your plate.
Join us at Shibui and let your taste buds explore the symphony of flavors that define authentic Japanese cuisine.
